Main Purpose: You will be able to direct a classical music ensemble. It may be a duet, a trio or a quartet. As the conductor, you will get the opportunity of choosing which ensemble you want to conduct;

  • Duet / Beethoven's Spring Sonata for piano and violin, 1st Movement excerpt.
  • Trio / Beethoven's Trio de l'Archiduc for piano, violin and cello, 2nd Movement excerpt.
  • Quartet / Mozart's Dissonance Quartet for violins, viola and cello, 2nd Movement excerpt.

The game is easy, you must place your hands in the designated space for each instrument depending on which ensemble you choose (you may find out more in the "How To" section in the game) upon the arrival of the instructions
